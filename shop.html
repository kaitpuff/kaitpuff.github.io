No returns or refunds are available at the moment.

shipping information

All items are made by hand, which means that every piece will be unique. The item you receive won't look exactly like the item pictured.

All items are shipped out on Mondays. Any orders places after 8pm on Sunday will be shipped the following week.

All orders are shipped with 2-day shipping. You will receive an email with a tracking number once an item is shipped.

item availability

Item availability is updated on the website. In the rare occasion that an item is out of stock and it is not stated on the website, you will receive an email stating when the item will be back in stock and it will be shipped immediately.

Email hi@kaitcartmell.com with any questions or concerns.
